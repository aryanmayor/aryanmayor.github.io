About me

Aryan Mayor is a young innovator with a passion for tinkering. His passion stemmed from his youth when his father noticed his ability to think outside the box and bought him a Lego robotics kit. For the first time, he was able to focus his creative energy on building and programming things. He would build different robots that could climb stairs, follow paths, and navigate the floor of his house.

His hunger for learning encouraged him to build computers, drones, and even a muscle controlled wheelchair. Aryan is an innovator at The Knowledge Society. They foster an environment where young innovators can develop their skills to integrate themselves in a professional workspace. TKS has enabled him to fine tune his skills in Computer Aided Design to innovate in the fields of 3D and 4D printing.

Aryan believes that a healthy lifestyle is the key to success. In his free time, he rock climbs to strengthen his body and clear his mind. He enjoys spending time with his friends while maintaining a strong work-life balance. If you'd like to know more about me, feel free to reach out at aryan.mayor@gmail.com.

3D Modeling

Currently, Aryan is specializing in 3D modeling. He enjoys the creative aspect of computer modeling as the only limitation is his mind. CAD or Computer Aided Design allows him to rapidly design things that he could use in his day to day life. With a few hours and a 3D printer, he can test these mockups and improve their design until they suit his needs. Aryan believes that 3D printing will change the traditional design process.
